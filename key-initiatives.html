Key Initiatives

Strategic priorities driving U.S.-DRC collaboration in mining, infrastructure, and industrialization.

Public-Private Consortium Framework

Establishing a joint U.S.-DRC industrial consortium to facilitate cross-border investment, transparency, and collaboration in mining and industrial projects.

Value-Added Mineral Processing

Developing local capabilities for refining cobalt, lithium, and copper to increase value retention in the DRC and reduce raw exports.

Pilot Projects Deployment

Launching 3–5 flagship initiatives in refining, logistics, and subcontracting to showcase replicable, scalable investment models.

Legal & Regulatory Structuring

Guided by international law expert Me Benoît Le Bars, ensuring solid joint ventures, arbitration safeguards, and OHADA-compliant frameworks.

Geostrategic Mineral Access

Securing critical mineral supply chains for U.S. industries while strengthening DRC’s positioning in the global energy transition economy.

International Partnership Engagement

Aligning with entities like Tesla, USAID, US DFC, and the U.S. Chamber of Commerce to integrate capital, ESG standards, and innovation.
