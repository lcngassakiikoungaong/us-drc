Welcome to Congo Industrial Consortium

Building a Secure Future Through Critical Minerals and U.S.–DRC Partnership

The Congo Industrial Consortium, led by Mayele Solutions in Washington D.C., connects American innovation with Congolese opportunity to secure critical mineral supply chains, promote stability, and empower local economies.

Who We Are

The Congo Industrial Consortium is a public–private partnership spearheaded by Mayele Solutions, with legal support from Lazareff Le Bars. We aim to structure a transformative economic alliance between the U.S. and the Democratic Republic of Congo, anchored in critical minerals like cobalt, copper, and lithium.

What We Do

    Promote secure, transparent mining investments
    Support industrialization in the DRC
    Advocate for bilateral cooperation and trade

Mission & Vision

A future where responsible mining fuels economic growth, peace, and supply chain security for both Africa and the United States and to forge a sustainable, transparent U.S.–DRC partnership in critical minerals through investment, legal excellence, and local empowerment.
